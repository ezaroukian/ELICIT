Terrorist plot:

WHO:
The who is a group (e.g., "Blue group").

WHAT:
The what is a type of target (e.g., "embassy", "religious school", "dignitary").

WHERE:
The where is the country in which the attack will take place (e.g., "Alphaland").

WHEN:
The when is the month, day, and hour on which the attack will occur (e.g., "December 15, at 3:00am").
